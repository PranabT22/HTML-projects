About

Our dog daycare and boarding centre provides the highest quality of care to dogs in Adelaide.

Our vision is to spread the word about new-age approaches to understanding dog behaviour. Dogwise Daycare recognises that every dog is an individual with its own way of seeing the world. We are open 7 days a week (Mon-Fri: 7 am-6:30 pm | Sat-Sun: 9 am-5 pm), allowing you to plan your week without any disturbances and drop off your pet when you need it.
